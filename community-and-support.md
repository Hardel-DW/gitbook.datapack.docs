# Community & Support

### <mark style="color:orange;">Connect With Us</mark>

Join the Voxel community across our social platforms to stay updated on the latest developments, share your experiences, and connect with other players:

* **Discord**: Join our active community where you can chat directly with developers, get support, and suggest new features
* **Twitter**: Follow for news, updates, and sneak peeks of upcoming content
* **GitHub**: Check out our open-source projects and contribute to development
* **The Discord:** [https://discord.gg/TAmVFvkHep](https://discord.gg/TAmVFvkHep)

### <mark style="color:orange;">Support Voxel</mark>

Voxel is a passion project created to enhance your Minecraft experience. If you enjoy our datapacks and tools, consider supporting development through:

* Patreon: Get exclusive benefits, early access to new features, and help shape the future of Voxel
* Donations: One-time contributions are always appreciated and help fund ongoing development
* Marketplace: Purchase premium resources to directly support our work

Every contribution helps us create better content and tools for the community!
