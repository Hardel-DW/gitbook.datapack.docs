# Compatibility with other mods

## <mark style="color:orange;">Compatibility</mark>

This enchantment datapack/mod is compatible with all other packs containing enchantments.



## <mark style="color:orange;">Possibly Affected Enchantement</mark>

&#x20;However, it alters certain Vanilla files, particularly block reward files. If another pack modifies these files, it may affect the Auto Smelt enchantment, which may not function on the files altered by another pack.
