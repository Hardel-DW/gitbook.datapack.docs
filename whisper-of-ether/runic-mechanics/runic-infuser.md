# Runic Infuser

## <mark style="color:orange;">Overview</mark>

The **Runic Infuser** transforms blank runes into stat-infused runes. Place a rune inside to receive it back with a random statistic.

## <mark style="color:orange;">How It Works</mark>

1. Place a **blank rune** (any tier) inside
2. The infuser processes it automatically
3. Receive the rune back with a **random statistic** attached

No items needed, fully automated! Perfect for filling your inventory with runes and applying them at the Runic Table.

---

## <mark style="color:orange;">Obtaining</mark>

Craft in a standard crafting table with:
- **Logs** (any type, surrounding)
- **Blank Rune** (center)
