# Runic Forge

## <mark style="color:orange;">Overview</mark>

The **Runic Forge** crafts runes using specific recipes. It provides access to all rune tiers and the **Echo Shard** needed for tier 3 runes.

---

## <mark style="color:orange;">Obtaining</mark>

Craft in a standard crafting table with:
- **8x Deepslate Tiles**
- **1x Nether Star**

---

## <mark style="color:orange;">Rune Recipes</mark>

6 recipes are available in the Runic Forge:
- 5 recipes for main rune tiers (Rune, Reinforced, Echo, Nether, Dragon)
- 1 recipe for **Echo Shard** (Warden-themed, used in tier 3 runes)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Automation</mark>

Place a chest on any of the 5 faces to automate rune production:
- **Top face**: Item input
- **Other faces**: Output and resource organization

Perfect for large-scale rune farming!
