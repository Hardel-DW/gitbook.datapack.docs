# Runic Mechanics

## <mark style="color:orange;">Overview</mark>

The runic system is the core of Whisper of Ether, enabling equipment customization through runes. Each rune modifies your gear's statistics, with mechanics inspired by Dofus where optimization involves trade-offs.

***

## <mark style="color:orange;">Core Pages</mark>

### [Runes](runes.md)

Complete reference of all available runes, their effects, tiers, and detailed statistical tables.

### [Runic Table](runic-table.md)

Apply runes to equipment and modify statistics. Learn about the Well system, success outcomes, and stat management.

### [Runic Forge](runic-forge.md)

Craft runes using specific recipes. Access all tiers and the Echo Shard. Supports automation for large-scale production.

### [Runic Infuser](runic-infuser.md)

Transform blank runes into stat-infused runes with random attributes. Fully automated process.
