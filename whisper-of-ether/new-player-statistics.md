# New Player Statistics

## <mark style="color:orange;">Multi Jump</mark>

**Rune of Multi Jump** — Jump again while in the air! Stack this stat to perform up to 5 jumps without touching the ground. Perfect for reaching high areas and evading attacks.

* **Base Value**: 1 (default 1 jump)
* **Min Value**: 0
* **Max Value**: 2,147,483,647 (unlimited scaling)

***

## <mark style="color:orange;">Critical Rate</mark>

**Rune of Critical Rate** — Increases your probability to trigger a critical hit. Higher values mean more frequent critical strikes. Pair with Critical Damage for devastating combos.

* **Base Value**: 1.0 (baseline)
* **Min Value**: 0.0
* **Max Value**: 1024.0

***

## <mark style="color:orange;">Critical Damage</mark>

**Rune of Critical Damage** — Amplifies damage dealt when you land a critical hit. The multiplier applies on top of your base damage, making this stat essential for burst damage builds.

* **Base Value**: 1.0 (no multiplier)
* **Min Value**: 0.0
* **Max Value**: 1024.0

***

## <mark style="color:orange;">Omnivampirism</mark>

**Rune of Omnivampirism** — Steal life from enemies when dealing damage. Heals you over time (3 seconds duration) based on the damage dealt. Works with all damage types.

* **Base Value**: 1.0 (no healing)
* **Min Value**: 0.0
* **Max Value**: 10.0

***

## <mark style="color:orange;">Omnivampirism Rate</mark>

**Rune of Omnivampirism Rate** — Controls the trigger frequency of life steal. Higher values mean more reliable healing procs. Essential for sustain-focused builds.

* **Base Value**: 1.0 (baseline)
* **Min Value**: 0.0
* **Max Value**: 1024.0

***

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pickup Area Size</mark>

**Rune of Pickup Area Size** — Expands your item collection radius, allowing you to vacuum nearby items from further away. Great for farming and gathering without leaving drops behind.

* **Base Value**: 1.0 (default range)
* **Min Value**: 0.0
* **Max Value**: 10.0
