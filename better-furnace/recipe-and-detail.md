**Recipe:**

- Surrounding: 8x Iron Blocks

## <mark style="color:orange;">Tips for Progression</mark>

- **Start with Iron** — Readily available and provides a solid early boost
- **Plan ahead** — Each tier requires a lot of the previous tier's material
- **Emerald is the sweet spot** — Offers excellent speed without extreme farming
- **Extreme requires planning** — Need multiple Wither defeats to gather enough Nether Stars
- **Mix & match** — Use different furnaces strategically based on your needs
